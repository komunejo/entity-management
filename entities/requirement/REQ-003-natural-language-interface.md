title: Natural language stays the collaboration interface
status: open
priority: must
date: 2026-07-12
tags: [core, interface]
---

Humans and agents collaborate in prose, not in SQL, Python, or HTML. The goal is not to move the collaboration into a structured language but to make the structure implicit in the prose explicit and verifiable *underneath*, without changing the working interface. The agent maintains the records as part of the workflow; the human keeps speaking their language.
